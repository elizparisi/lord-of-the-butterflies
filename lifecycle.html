Life Cycle Timeline

  • EGG

    3 to 8 Days

    Monarch butterfly eggs are laid on the underside of the milkweed leaves during the spring and summer months. Once they hatch, they feed on the milkweed plant.

  • CATERPILLAR

    9 to 14 Days

    The growing caterpillars have five stages, called instars. With each instar the caterpillar molts-shedding it's skin. The caterpillars main job during this time is to eat and grow.

  • CHRYSALIS

    8 to 15 Days

    The caterpillar spins a silk mat to hang from. It's during this stage that the wings and other adult organs form.

  • EMERGE (AND MIGRATE)

    6 to 9 Months

    Once the adult monarchs emerge, they will live about 2-6 weeks during summer. However, monarchs that migrate will live about 6-9 months, through the winter.
